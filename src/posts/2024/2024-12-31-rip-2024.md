Started out the year living temporarily in Fitzroy North, thanks to the kind and wonderful [Andrea](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrea-kirby-talenttable/). Found a new house pretty quickly, living in the eastern suburbs again (the third time in the same square kilometre, even). Got the kids into schools, found a new job (working with Nick and Kaitlin again üéâ), started getting settled back into Australia.

### October

Spent the month job hunting and marveling at how quickly [one man](https://www.theverge.com/2024/10/4/24262410/wordpress-fight-trademarks-open-source-mullenweg) can destroy years of goodwill (from those who have not met him personally, at any rate). Thankfully, the job hunting aspect was much less of a train wreck and I started a new role looking after recruiting for [Ferocia](https://ferocia.com.au) ‚ù§Ô∏è

### November

Cut out all my sources for US political news and I'm much happier for it. The election was like watching a wasted friend who's pissed their pants and is screaming obscenities at anyone who tries to help - I don't need to like your choices, but I don't need to stick around and watch the consequences either.

Spent Christmas with family, watched football, got more [Lego](https://www.lego.com/en-au/product/the-milky-way-galaxy-31212), relaxed, and generally decompressed after a year that was slightly less mad than the previous one, but still *a lot*.
